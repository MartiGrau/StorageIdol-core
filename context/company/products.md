# Products

Three commercial modules, sold independently or as a bundle.

---

## Module 1 — Customer Service 24/7

**What it does:** Unified agent layer that answers inbound queries, schedules property visits, qualifies inquiries, and escalates to a human when needed.

**Channels:** Voice (inbound/outbound) + WhatsApp

**Key capabilities:**
- Multilingual (native via ElevenLabs + base LLM)
- FAQ answering with client-specific knowledge base
- Visit scheduling with calendar integration
- Human escalation triggers (sentiment, intent, explicit request)
- Full conversation logging

**Sold as:** Monthly subscription per active channel + setup fee

---

## Module 2 — Lead Management & Nurturing

**What it does:** Captures leads from any source, qualifies them automatically, runs a nurturing sequence, schedules a visit, and hands off to sales only when the lead is warm.

**Trigger sources:** Web form, missed call, WhatsApp inbound

**Sequence logic:**
- Immediate first contact (< 2 min from capture)
- Qualification questions (budget, timeline, intent)
- Spaced follow-up reminders with natural tone variation
- Visit scheduling
- CRM update + human handoff with full context

**Sold as:** Per-lead pricing (volume tiers) + setup fee

---

## Module 3 — Debt Collection & Recovery

**What it does:** Detects unpaid invoices via CRM integration and executes a staged outreach sequence with tone escalation and integrated payment options.

**Default staging:**
- D+3: Friendly WhatsApp reminder
- D+10: AI outbound call (informational tone)
- D+20: Negotiation call with payment plan options
- D+30: Escalate to human agent with full history

**Key capabilities:**
- CRM-triggered automation (no manual list uploads needed)
- Integrated payment gateway link in messages
- Full audit log of all contact attempts (regulatory compliance)
- Tone and script customization per stage per client

**Sold as:** % of recovered amount + monthly platform fee

---

## Bundle pricing

Clients that take all three modules receive a discount on the monthly platform fee and a single onboarding process. Cross-module data (a lead who becomes a tenant who later has a debt) flows automatically between modules.
